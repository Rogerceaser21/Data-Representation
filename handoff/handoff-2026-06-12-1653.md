## 4 · UI direction (decided, from the designcourse "Fable 5" video)

Igor watched a designcourse video (youtu.be/tSg3FAdWvzI) of this model one-shotting award-grade UIs and wants the dashboard "spectacular, very schmick when the data is manipulated". Watched via /watch (80 frames + captions). Locked direction:

- **GSAP + ScrollTrigger as the animation backbone**: filter changes morph charts (no redraws), count-ups, FLIP card reflows, staggered chip entrances, split-text headings, fast subtle easing.
- **D3 or ECharts** for charts, custom AIS theme.
- **three.js for exactly ONE signature moment** (candidate: login/landing scene), never for data views.
- **Deck design language throughout** + the established green-to-red judgement palette.
- The video's most relevant lesson is the Craigslist demo: modernize while staying type-heavy/dense. Dashboards are density-with-polish, not 3D gimmicks.
- A focused research pass on reference education dashboards is still owed at board-design time.
