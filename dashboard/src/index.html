Build history
v0.22New Snapshot board, now the landing view. A governance "at a glance" picture of the June R3 sweep with a Primary and Kindy / Secondary / Compare toggle. Each phase shows lessons observed, teachers seen, average progress (best evidence per teacher) on a ramp gauge and a six-point distribution, a What is working and Where to focus next summary, and three insights for each drawn from the inspectors' written evidence. Compare puts the two phases side by side with a diverging distribution chart. Teacher subschool (section) is now populated in Supabase from the staff register, so the split is live data, not hard-coded.
v0.21Dashboard now reads LIVE from Supabase. It paints the baked snapshot first (instant + offline fallback), then upgrades to live data via a read-only Supabase RPC and re-renders, so edits in Supabase show on refresh. Login-less for now (the password gate is the soft lock); proper @ais.ae sign-in comes later. If Supabase is unreachable it silently stays on the baked snapshot.
v0.20Masthead cleaned up: the Stars and theme buttons were removed from the top bar (both still live in Settings). A floating light/dark toggle, the same round sun/moon button as the R3 form, now sits in the top-right corner and is always available.
v0.19R3 Obs is now a fan-out menu of observation levels: Teacher Level (the working teacher search), Grade / Department Level, and School Level (which expands to Kindy, Lower Primary, Upper Primary, Whole Primary, Secondary). Teacher Level works as before; the other levels land on a styled placeholder for now (front-end only, to be connected to data later). The R3 Obs button stays as the menu trigger in every state.
v0.18Fixed the search: the search bar was leaking into the hub state (a class display rule was overriding the hidden attribute), so it appeared before R3 Obs was clicked and filtered a list that was not shown. Now the hidden attribute always wins, so the hub shows only R3 Obs and the search filters once you enter it. Each tab now has its own URL too: Observations is /#teachers, Settings is /#settings, Coverage is the default; the link changes, is bookmarkable, and back/forward work.
v0.17Observations tab tightened: the R3 Obs button and the teacher search now sit inline on the "Observations" header line (whichever state is active) instead of stacked in their own panels, reclaiming the wasted space. The coloured A / B index letters were removed from the Coverage and Observations tabs (and the redundant R3 from the button), and the kicks are now just "Coverage" and "Observations".
v0.16The Teacher tab is now the Observations tab, a small workflow. It opens on a hub with an "R3 Obs" button; clicking it shows the teacher search (moved off the Coverage page). No observation detail shows until a teacher is picked, after which only the search bar stays at the top with the selected teacher's detail below. Typing again re-shows the list to switch teacher. Back returns to Coverage.
v0.15All-observations SEAS bar refined: the six cards are now joined into one seamless bar (no gaps or dividers), the numbers are dropped (words only), and the bar bottom-aligns with the gauge circles so it never sits below them. The observation dots now reserve real height above the bar, so the card grows to hold tall dot stacks instead of overlapping the headers.
v0.14All-observations Spectrum restyled as the SEAS scale: the smooth gradient bar is replaced by six SEAS cards (colour swatch + judgement word + number, centred), one per point. The observation dots still float above and the average triangle below. Only the All-observations card changed; the per-session cards keep the smooth gradient bar.
v0.13All-observations card is now symmetric: the Student Progress gauge sits to the left of the Spectrum and a new Teaching gauge to the right, with three matched headers (Student Progress | All observations | Teaching) above their columns. The Teaching gauge is an exact horizontal mirror of Student Progress (gap opens left, fills clockwise) and shows only the Teaching judgement (criterion r3_05) as the teacher-wide average, always reading all observations, just like Student Progress.
v0.12Spectrum average marker now points up at the bar (it was pointing down, away from the scale) and is 25% larger, so the teacher-wide average reads as a clear pointer to its position on the scale.
v0.11All-observations card balanced: "All observations" and "Student Progress" are now matched bold headers; the Student Progress gauge sits up and its circle centre lines up with the middle of the Spectrum bar (independent of how many dots stack), and the card is trimmed to only the height it needs.
v0.10All-observations card tidied: the legend and the Average-of-observations word are removed, the Student Progress label is bold, and the gauge sits up beside the Spectrum. Freeze fix done properly: the Spectrum and gauge entrances are now CSS animations on a resting-visible base (real-time timeline, not requestAnimationFrame), so a backgrounded or restored tab can no longer leave the dots or gauge blank.
v0.9The two top centerpieces are gone. The Spectrum and the All-observations bar (which showed the same data) are now one full-width Spectrum inside the All-observations card, with a Student Progress gauge beside it. The gauge shows only the Progress judgement and always reads all observations. Selecting a session rings its dot on the Spectrum. A pageshow/visibilitychange finalize stops the gauge and spectrums from being stranded blank after the tab is backgrounded or restored.
v0.8Card refinements: any click on a card selects it; the Inspection and Skills arrows sit beside their titles, gently animate, and open both columns together; the Spectrum is slimmer and full width with judgement words at the body text size; the section headers match the Observation Notes header.
v0.7Observation card redesign: collapsible Inspection and Skills sections, full-width Spectrum, a condensed action bar (teacher name, Observation Notes, and a link to the locked R3 record), and an expander revealing notes, strengths, and areas to develop.
v0.6All-observations card reflects the selected session.
v0.5Session-card redesign with subject and subject area, purple selection ring, restored animations.
v0.4Best skill wins per observation; teacher overall is the average of those per-observation scores.
v0.3Card ramps match the Spectrum, with one colour per skill.
