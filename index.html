How ratings are read

Rating scale

A single shared scale across all observation types — formal, walkthrough, probation, ESCM, OTP & APR. Outstanding and Very Good are the two "at or above" bands; Sound meets expectation; the lower bands flag where targeted support is needed.

Level & descriptor
O OutstandingConsistently demonstrates a very high level of practice, applied independently across a wide range of contexts and observation types.
VG Very GoodDemonstrates a high level of practice, applied independently across most contexts.
S SoundDemonstrates a sound level of practice, applied in some contexts; meets expectation.
D DevelopingPractice is developing; targeted coaching or follow-up support recommended.
SR Support RequiredSignificant support required to develop the area of practice; structured intervention.
IE Insufficient EvidencePractice level not yet able to be established for this observation cycle.

Teacher Observation · Pitch v0.13 05 / 05 · The build
From paper to pretty website

How we build this

01
Job One

Form conversions

Paper & PDF observation forms become structured digital forms. One source of truth — no double-handling, no lost notes.
  • Digital SPEA, walkthrough, probation, ESCM, OTP & APR forms.
  • Built for speed: observers complete during the observation, not after.
  • Tablet- and phone-friendly; works offline, syncs on connect.
  • Same field structure across all forms feeds the same database.
Step 1 of 3 · Collection
02
Job Two

Central database

Every observation, walkthrough and self-assessment lands in one place. Per-teacher, per-department, per-school — queryable, exportable.
  • Single source of truth for all observation data.
  • Role-based access: SLT, observers, individual teachers.
  • Audit log on every record — who, when, what changed.
  • Export-ready for KHDA, accreditation and federation reviews.
Step 2 of 3 · Storage
03
Job Three

Pretty Data Representation

The showcase layer. Same look-and-feel as the academic reports, drilling from school → department → teacher.
  • Per-teacher dashboards (slide 3 of this deck).
  • Per-department views (slide 4 of this deck).
  • School-level rollup for SLT & review-readiness.
  • Branded, password-gated, exportable as PDF for reviewers.
